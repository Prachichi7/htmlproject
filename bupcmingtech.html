  • Improved Tracking and Controllers: Enhanced Tracking: The Quest 3 is said to have improved tracking capabilities, providing more precise and responsive in-VR movements. Imagine viewing 3D models in your living room, attending virtual meetings in a customized workspace, or even having virtual pets interact with your physical environment.

Content Library: The Quest 3 has access to the ever-growing library of Meta Quest games and applications, providing a variety of VR experiences for entertainment, exercise, creativity, and more.
